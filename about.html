What we do

Entrepreneur Accelerator Program is a registered non-profit organisation that was founded by bright young minds. The organisation strives to make a meaningful contribution to the ventures of historically disadvantaged South African entrepreneurs. The organisation empowers students and local emerging businesses that are in and around Cape Town through a hands on consulting engagement. The key to success is hard work, creativity, and a strong focus on working as a team. The mission of the organisation is derived from the new hope for South Africa. The organisation understand that the possibility that the social difficulties that we face as a nation should be dealt with by the youth and the youth must have platforms where they can contribute to practical solutions to the problems our country faces.

The program take on professional students and turns them into professional consultants. We introduce students to the South African business context, township entrepreneurship, the basics of the consulting process, and practical approaches to addressing managerial issues in emerging enterprises. Moreover, we expose students to practical approaches in helping entrepreneurs to run successful businesses through innovative seminars. Students are grouped into small consulting teams and each team works with two emerging enterprises. Consulting teams are expected to identify and prioritize the needs of the ventures and make meaningful progress in addressing those needs with tangible deliverables that can be implemented by the entrepreneur.

This is not about analysis for the sake of analysis. It is about tangible deliverables for our clients. And it is about getting those deliverables implemented in the business. Achieving all of this requires that we build a relationship of trust, credibility and integrity. We believe in our professional consultants and what they can achieve.
